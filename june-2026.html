Overview
The Big Picture

Teacher spotlights dominated — and the data proves why.

June was the strongest growth month since January — 51 new profiles, up from 34 in May and 22 in April. Net list movement: +19 (51 subscribes, 32 unsubscribes). Teacher spotlight content ran all month and drove the highest engagement of any content type.

The standout story was teacher spotlights as a recurring format. June saw 4 teacher spotlight campaigns sent to the full list, plus a Teacher Showcase Roundup on June 30. Open rates ranged from 41.6% to 54.1% on 1,000+ recipient sends. The June 30 Roundup delivered the highest click rate of the month — 42 clicks, 3.9% CTR on 1,075 delivered.

The "Beyond the Classroom" campaign (Jun 11) was the strongest single full-list send — 60.2% open rate on 922 delivered, 15 clicks. Content that shows school culture rather than selling admissions continues to outperform.

The welcome series improved meaningfully in June. Email 1 held at 64.3% open rate. Emails 3 and 4 both improved — 54.1% and 59.5% — after months of underperformance. The pattern in the data strongly supports adding teacher spotlight content to welcome emails 3–5.

LTN flow volume grew. Track A Email 1 delivered to 12 families at 50% OR. The funnel is building momentum.

💡 What Five Journeys Tell Us

Long-nurture leads (Crystal, Matthew, Yingying — 15–23 months) all converted within days of receiving teacher spotlight content. Fast-cycle leads (Patrice, Dana — 0–17 days) converted on the welcome series and event path before teacher spotlights reached them. The data makes the case: teacher spotlights belong in the welcome series for families who don't convert quickly. The right content, earlier, shortens the nurture window.
