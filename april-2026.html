Overview
The Big Picture

A steady month — with a milestone worth highlighting: the Long-Term Nurture Flow went live.

April was a steady month with strong campaign variety and a milestone worth highlighting: the Long-Term Nurture Flow went live. After the welcome series, families now enter one of three personalized tracks based on their start year and event attendance — the segmentation infrastructure we've been building toward since February.

List growth remained soft — 22 new profiles, consistent with March's 24. This is the second consecutive quiet month and likely reflects the off-peak event calendar. The Discovery Walks in April (Apr 10, Apr 27) and the May 1 Open House should carry forward into May's growth number.

The standout performance in April was the Spark Social Oogubi Wonderland event email — 53% open rate, 9.2% click rate on 510 recipients. Event-adjacent content continues to outperform general campaigns for this audience. The April Newsletter held strong at 52.6% open rate across 1,052 recipients.

The Apr 30 "Why Families Choose Hiba" email came in lower — 20.8% open rate — but this email was sent at the very end of the month and continues to accumulate opens. The send was intentional: planting a value-driven email before the May 1 Open House.

New — Automated Flow
Long-Term Nurture Flow — Live
Launched Late April ✓

The Long-Term Nurture Flow launched in late April. This is the three-track system we designed to pick up where the welcome series leaves off — taking families on a personalized path based on their start year and how engaged they've been.

How It Works
Track A1
Families interested in Fall 2026 who haven't attended an event yet. Goal: get them to a Discovery Walk or Open House.
Track A2
Families who attended an event but haven't filled out their Ravenna form. Goal: move them to complete their form and take the next step.
Track B
Families interested in Fall 2027 or later. Goal: build a long-term relationship until they're ready to act.
Early Data (First Few Days)

Track A2 Email 1 (SamYgx) has delivered to 4 families and already recorded 4 unique opens — 100% open rate in the first few days. Early signal is strong. Full performance data will appear in the May report.

This is the technical foundation we've been working toward. With the welcome series running, Ravenna integration syncing data automatically, and the LTN flow now live — the full funnel is operational for the first time.
